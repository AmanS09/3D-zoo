Lion

Lions, scientifically known as Panthera leo, are majestic big cats in Africa known for their magnificent manes, behavior and predator status. They usually live in prides consisting of lionesses and their cubs led by a strong male. Lions are carnivores; They hunt many large animals such as antelope, zebra and buffalo. Their combination of hunting skills, strength, and speed make them great hunters. These animals are known for their unique sounds that can be heard from great distances and serve as a means of communication within the area of ​​interest. Although powerful and respected in the animal kingdom, lions face constant threats such as habitat loss, human-wildlife conflicts and poaching, making conservation important for lions to survive in the wild.
