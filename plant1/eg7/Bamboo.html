Lucky Bamboo

Lucky bamboo is a popular indoor plant said to bring good luck and prosperity. Also known as dracaena or ribbon plant. The botanical name of Lucky Bamboo is Dracena sanderiana. Lucky Bamboo originates from Southeast Asia and has been used in Feng Shui for 5,000 years. It is said that this plant brings health, love and luck to its owner. Various parts of the plant represent earth, wood, water, metal and fire. The stems can be shaped into spirals by controlling the amount of sunlight they receive. Lucky Bamboo is easy to care for and can grow up to one centimeter per month. The plant has slightly twisted grey-green leaves that can grow up to 23 cm (9 in) long.
