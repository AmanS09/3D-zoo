Magnoloia soulangeana(Saucer Magnolia)

The saucer magnolia (Magnolia x soulangeana) is a hybrid flowering plant that is a cross between Magnolia denudata and Magnolia liliiflora. It is a deciduous tree with large, fragrant flowers that bloom in early to mid-spring. The flowers are goblet-shaped and can be white, pink, or purple. The leaves are mid-green and turn golden brown in the fall before falling. The saucer magnolia can grow to be 20 to 30 feet tall and 25 feet wide. It has a lifespan of more than 20 years. The saucer magnolia is also known as the tulip tree, Chinese magnolia, or saucer magnolia. It prefers full sun or partial shade in well-drained soil. It dislikes dry or alkaline soil.
