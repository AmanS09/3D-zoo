Alien Flora

Alien flora are plants that have been introduced to a region outside their native range by humans. These plants are often brought in as exotics, either intentionally or unintentionally. They become naturalized when they are able to reproduce and spread without direct assistance from people.
Alien flora can threaten biological diversity and cause economic or environmental harm. They are often herbs, most of which are annuals. The majority of alien plant species are of American origin, followed by those of European and Asiatic origin
The alien flora comprise of 771 species, including 375 cultivated and 396 naturalized. Annual herbs from Southern America showed higher naturalization probability. Total alien plant richness was best explained by the average annual rainfall. Naturalized plant richness was best predicted by total traffic length.
